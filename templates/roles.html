Roles & Permissions
Five role levels control what each staff member can see and do. Assign roles when adding staff accounts in app.py or via the Staff Manager.
admin
Full access to everything including delete, sell, staff management and exports.
manager
Can edit, sell, add vehicles and export. Cannot delete or manage staff.
sales
Can view, edit vehicles, add new stock and assign key tags. Cannot sell or delete.
service
View-only access plus key tag assignment. Cannot edit vehicle details.
viewer
Read-only access to inventory. No editing, no keys, no admin.

How to Assign Roles — Edit app.py

Find the _staff_accounts dict in app.py and add accounts with the desired role:

# In app.py — _staff_accounts dict _staff_accounts = { "admin": {"name": "Admin", "role": "admin", "password": ADMIN_PASSWORD}, "staff": {"name": "Staff", "role": "staff", "password": ACCESS_PASSWORD}, # Add your team below: "john": {"name": "John Smith", "role": "sales", "password": "John2024!"}, "sarah": {"name": "Sarah Jones", "role": "manager", "password": "Sarah2024!"}, "tech": {"name": "Tech Team", "role": "service", "password": "Tech2024!"}, "readonly":{"name": "Read Only", "role": "viewer", "password": "View2024!"}, }

After editing, push app.py to GitHub — Railway will auto-deploy within ~2 minutes.
