Staff Management

Manage accounts, roles and personal contact numbers. Each staff member's number appears on spec sheets they share with customers.

💡
How personalised share links work: When a staff member opens a vehicle and copies the share link, it includes ?ref=username automatically. The customer sees that staff member's name and phone number on the spec sheet — not the general dealership number.

If a staff member loses their phone: Just update their number here. The link they already shared still works — the customer will now call the new number. No links need to be re-shared.
